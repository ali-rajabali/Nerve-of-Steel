#This is a program to execute the Nerve of Steel Game

Run Nerve of Steel Game
	Put chairs together
	Bring players to chairs
	Say "Players stand"
	Run a timer for a random number of seconds between the range of 5 and 25 seconds
	UNTIL timer is running, sit down
		Say "Time up. Last to sit down wins"
		UNTIL last person does not sit down, continue game
